About the Show

Three Bears. One Stack. Infinite Fun! 🐻

We Bare Bears is an American animated television series created by Daniel Chong for Cartoon Network. It follows three bear brothers — Grizzly, Panda, and Ice Bear — as they attempt to integrate into human society in the San Francisco Bay Area with humor, heart, and a whole lot of snacks.

📅 2015–2019 🎬 Cartoon Network 🏆 Emmy Nominated 🐻 4 Seasons 🌎 140+ Episodes

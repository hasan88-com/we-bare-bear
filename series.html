We Bare Bears follows three brother bears — Grizzly, Panda, and Ice Bear — who live together in a cave in the San Francisco Bay Area. Each episode chronicles their often-disastrous attempts to fit in with human society: going viral on social media, visiting food trucks, making friends, and finding their place in a city that wasn't quite built for bears. Created by Daniel Chong, the series aired on Cartoon Network from July 2015 to May 2019 across four beloved seasons.
